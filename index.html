About

Sparkle is my personal reading journal. A small place where I keep track of books that inspired me, taught me something new, or simply stayed in my mind long after the last page.

I created this project to build a digital bookshelf that grows with me over time. Every book here represents an idea, a lesson, or a story worth remembering.
